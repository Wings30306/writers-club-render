Guidelines for Appropriate Conduct

General Guidelines

  1. Always be respectful. Basically, the Golden Rule applies here: You wouldn’t want someone to treat you badly, so don’t do it to others either.
  2. Cyberbullying will not be tolerated and will result in an instant ban.
  3. Signing up is required for reading adult-rated stories, posting stories and posting feedback. We won’t reveal your private information (email address, date of birth) to anyone else.

Posting your stories

All stories are welcome here, however we would ask you to keep the following guidelines into account:

  1. Please clearly rate your story, especially if it’s R-rated, 18+, NSFW. If you’re not sure what the rating for your story is, check the Ratings Guidelines.
  2. If your story is a fan fic, please add the required disclaimers as to copyright. A standard disclaimer is pre-programmed in the story submission section; however you are allowed to create a custom one instead. No disclaimer is required for original stories.

Feedback

You'll find a Feedback button after every story. Giving feedback is a nice boost for the author and as such, you're very welcome to tell them what you thought. Please be kind:

  1. The Feedback section is not the place to push your own stories. Please limit the comments only to the story you’re commenting on. Do not ask others to read your stories just because you commented on theirs.
  2. Constructive criticism is welcome, destructive criticism is not. No comments saying: "Ugh, this story is crap!" please!
  3. Likewise, spam messages will be removed and may result in a ban if warnings are unheeded.
  4. If you feel a certain story has been wrongly rated / categorised, please use the report button to allow moderators to check it. The story will NOT be removed while being checked, but if a report is deemed accurate, the author will be contacted to request they adjust the categories as needed.

If we can all keep these simple guidelines in mind, this can stay a welcoming place for all. Thank you in advance for your cooperation!

Happy reading / writing!
